# ham-technician-notes
Notes on taking the amateur radio level 1 technician license exam. 

## Overview
- Learn more: https://www.arrl.org/getting-your-technician-license 
- Exam Requirement: 35-question Technician Written Exam (Element 2).
- Privileges: All VHF/UHF Amateur bands (frequencies above 30 MHz).
    Limited operations in certain HF bands.
- Expiration: US licenses are good for 10 years before renewal and anyone may hold one except a representative of a foreign government
